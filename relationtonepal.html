My Relation to Nepal

Me!:)

Relatives! :)

I have many relatives in Nepal. Currently, I have two uncles and 2 aunts living in Kathmandu. I also have cousins in the city of Itahari. One of my cousins is Rosma Niraula. She is very nice. Rosma didi is the daughter of my father's older brother. All my relatives are great! I don't have many friends in Nepal though. Many people think that Nepal is all poor and small. Friends :)

I don't really have any friends in Nepal. Nepal is full of good people, but I don't really get to know anyone.
