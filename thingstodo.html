Things to do in Nepal!

Katmandu!

Katmandu is a very great place to be in Nepal. There are many things to do there. Pahupatinath Temple is a great example. There you can see and respect our gods. The temple is very big and really nicely built. It is wonderful and very peaceful to go there. Boudhanath Stupa is one of the most popular tourist sites in the Kathmandu area. Since 1979, Boudha Stupa is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Sagarmatha! (Reach for the stars!)

Sagarmatha is another one of the most visted places in Nepal. It is the home of the biggest mountain in the whole world, Mount Everest. Mount Everest is 8,848.86 meters. That is 29,032 feet!
