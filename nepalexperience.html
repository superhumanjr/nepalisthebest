My Nepal Experience!

Me in Nepal!

I have been to Nepal only twice in my life. One is when I used to live there and the other is when we went there to do a pooja after my grandmother passed away.

Taking the Plane to Nepal

The second time we went to Nepal, we took the plane flight, Emirates. It was a nice long flight that took a very long period of time. During the flight, I watched a movie and stayed in my comfy seat. It was a great flight.

Arrival

We arrived in the capital city, Kathmandu. There, we stayed in our uncle's house. Later, my tholobaba came to my uncle's house. I saw the famous Pashupatinath Temple. It was so huge! The temple was very nicely structured. There were very big pillars in the temple.

Travels

After a week of staying in Kathmandu, My mother, sister, tholobaba, and I said farewell to my uncle and headed to Itahari. My father couldn't come because he had some work to do in Kathmandu. We arrived in Itahari in about a day. In Itahari I met one of my favorite cousins. In two days, my father joined us in Itahari. There my parents bought some land, and set up a shade to do the pooja. The pooja honored my late grandmother. The pooja lasted two days. After the pooja my family went to visit one of my father's brothers. I had some fun with another one of my cousins there. We didn't stay that much. After the visit, we went to my youngest aunt's house. There I met more of my cousins, who were great. After staying for three days we set off for my older aunt's house. There I met two of my other cousins. They too were great people. I found out that the younger cousin of the two was only younger than me by about two months. After all that, it was finally time to go home.

Coming back to America!

After a month's visit to Nepal, we finally came back home to America. We took the same airline, Emirates. The flight felt quicker then before. After a month's visit, we have stayed in America for 2 years. I think that next year we will probably go back. You never know!

The End!
